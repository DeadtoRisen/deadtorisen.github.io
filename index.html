About the project

Dead to Risen is a Christian rock project built on real stories: doubt, relapse, late-night drives, and the kind of grace that keeps chasing you anyway.

The songs hit hard but stay honest — mixing modern rock, subtle metal edges, and lyrics that point straight back to the cross.
